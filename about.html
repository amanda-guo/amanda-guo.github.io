Chapter 5: I’m Amanda, a Product Designer Based in Toronto
I’m a third year Computer Science student at the University of Waterloo. Currently, I’m designing for worker safety and accessibility at the WSIB Innovation Lab. On the side, I’m designing for social good at UW Blueprint. Previously, I was a Product Manager/UX Designer at Tecsys.

I’ve always believed there was something inherently human about tech and that’s what I intend to explore. Combining my passion for psychology, history and development, I hope to design inclusive products that will impact the lives of others.

Apart from design, catch me rock climbing, snowboarding and finding the latest mountains to hike. When I’m not being adventurous, I can be found reading a book or binging my favourite shows on Netflix. Learn more about my literary and literal adventures here.

Feel free to contact me at amanda.guo@uwaterloo.ca. I’m always open to chat!
